### About Our Lab

Welcome to the Soft Artificial Life Lab (SAL LAb.), led by **Prof. Seungmin Park** at Jeju National University

Our research focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Soft Computing, and Artificial Life. We aim to develop intelligent systems that mimic the adaptive and evolutionary characteristics of biological life. By leveraging advanced machine learning techniques and evolutionary algorithms, we explore new frontiers in autonomous systems, bio-inspired robotics, and intelligent data analysis.

---

### Research Interests

Our lab is dedicated to solving complex real-world problems through :

* **Artificial Life & Evolutionary Computation** : Designing algorithms inspired by biological evolution to optimize complex systems
* **Soft Computing** : Utilizing fuzzy logic, neural networks and probabilistic reasoning to handle uncertainty and imprecision in data.
* **Machine Learning & Deep Learning** : Developing robust models for pattern recognition, predictive modeling, and intelligent decision-making
* **Bio-inspired Systems** : Engineering Software and hardware architectures that exhibit life-like behaviors such as self-organization and adaptation

---

### Biography

**Prof. Seungmin Park** is an Associated Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Jeju National University. Before joining Jeju National University, he had significant research initiatives at Dongseo University, focusing on the convergence of AI and soft computing technologies. He is committed to fostering a creative research environment where students can explore the potential of artificial life to shape the future of technology.
